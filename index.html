Recipe for
Spaghetti with Mozzarella and Basil

Ingredients

3pounds fresh plum tomatoes, blanched, peeled, seeded and quartered, 3ounces olive oil, Salt and freshly ground pepper to taste, 1 pinch crushed red-pepper flakes1pound dry spaghetti2ounces

Preparations

  1. Cut the tomatoes crosswise. Set aside in a bowl.
  2. Heat 3 ounces olive oil in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat until it smokes slightly. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper and crushed red pepper.
  3. Chop the tomatoes with a potato masher until they are in fine chunks and all their liquid is released. Be sure they are chopped and crushed fine, for a semichunky sauce. Simmer for 25 minutes over medium heat.
  4. While the sauce simmers, heat the water for the pasta. Cook the spaghetti in salted water about half of the way cooked. Drain, reserving some of the water.
